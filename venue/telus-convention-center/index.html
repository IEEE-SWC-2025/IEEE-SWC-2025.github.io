World-Class Convention Center in the Heart of Calgary

Telus Convention Center

The Calgary Telus Convention Center is located in the heart of the city, surrounded by a variety of other hotels ranging from luxury to budget options. This proximity ensures that conference attendees have a range of accommodation choices within walking distance. The Calgary Telus Convention Center is close to the City Hall of Calgary and key attractions such as the Calgary Tower, Stephen Avenue Walk (a historic pedestrian mall), the Glenbow Museum, and the Arts Commons. These sites offer cultural and recreational activities for conference attendees during their free time. The centre is near various restaurants, bars, cafes, and shopping centers, providing ample opportunities for socializing and leisure.
