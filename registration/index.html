Registration Instructions for 2025 IEEE Smart World Congress (SWC 2025)

You are strongly encouraged to read the following notes before starting the registration process.

Full Conference Author Registration

Full Conference Author Registration is required for authors with accepted papers (each paper must have at least one author who completes the full registration either In-person or Online), and it includes the following:

  • Publication of one accepted paper in the proceedings
  • A copy of the proceedings the accepted paper belongs to
  • Access to all conference, Industry Day, workshops, tutorials, and special sessions
  • Program brochure and conference goodies
  • For full conference author registration (in-person), lunch, coffee breaks, and reception (banquet is not included and can be paid separately)
  • For papers to be published in IEEE proceedings, two extra pages are allowed, and each extra page costs USD $100

The Full Conference Author Registration fees are listed below:

The early-bird deadline for Full Conference Author Registration is June 30, 2025. The final deadline for Full Conference Author Registration is July 7, 2025. Please note that if your payment is not received by that date, the paper will be removed from the proceedings. This deadline will be strictly enforced.

For authors with multiple accepted papers, one full registration is valid for a maximum of 3 papers, with the 2nd and 3rd papers each requiring an additional USD $300. If a 4th paper is to be registered, the author must complete a new registration.

Full Conference Non-Author Registration

Full Conference Non-Author Registration fee applies when one author has already completed full registration for a paper, and additional authors of the same paper or other attendees wish to participate in the conference in person. The Full Conference Non-Author Registration fees are listed below:

Industry Day Tickets

The industry day (August 19) ticket is included in the registration. If you wish to participate in Industry Day only, the ticket is sold separately for USD $100.

Payment

Payment can be made by credit cards (Visa/Mastercard/American Express).
