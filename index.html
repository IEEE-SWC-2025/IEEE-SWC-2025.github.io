Join us at IEEE Smart World Congress 2025

We are thrilled to invite you to the IEEE Smart World Congress 2025, a premier event bringing together thought leaders and researchers in smart technologies. Held in the vibrant city of Calgary, Canada, from August 18-22, 2025, this conference provides a unique platform for interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation across fields such as IoT, AI, smart cities, cybersecurity, and computing. Attendees will gain insights from co-located conferences, keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities, all while experiencing Calgary’s renowned attractions and thriving tech ecosystem.

The smart world is transforming everyday life by integrating sensation, communication, computation, and intelligence into the objects and systems around us, making processes more efficient and enjoyable. This interconnected ecosystem of "smart things" holds tremendous potential to benefit humanity, but it also demands fairness and ethical stewardship. As we explore these innovations, research in ubiquitous and trustworthy smart world technologies continues to expand, addressing societal challenges and fostering meaningful impacts. Join us at IEEE SWC 2025 to shape the future of smart technologies together!

We look forward to seeing you in Calgary!
Sincerely, The 2025 IEEE SWC Organizing Committee

Explore Stunning Calgary and Its Scenic Surroundings

Calgary, Alberta, Canada, is a vibrant city nestled in the foothills of the Canadian Rockies, known for its dynamic mix of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure. As Alberta's largest city and a key economic hub, Calgary boasts a thriving energy industry, a growing tech sector, and a strong entrepreneurial spirit. The city is famous for the annual Calgary Stampede, a celebration of its western heritage, and offers stunning natural beauty, with easy access to the nearby Banff and Jasper national parks.
